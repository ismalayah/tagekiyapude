See Vanderpump Rules stars' biggest &amp; smallest engagement rings- as Lala Kent claims '$150K diamond'

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, June 5, 2024

THE cast of Vanderpump Rules has seen their fair share of bling over the years, amidst lavish engagements, over-the-top marriages, and messy splits.

From Katie Maloney's petite diamond to Lala Kent's massive "fake" gem, the Bravo stars have seen it all.

BRITTANY CARTWRIGHT & JAX TAYLOR

Jax Taylor got down on one knee in June of 2018 to propose to his on-and-off girlfriend Brittany Cartwright with a 3.14-carat cushion cut white diamond.

The traditional ring was a perfect fit for the Kentucky native, with a thin double halo of smaller diamonds surrounding the center stone.

The reality star spent a whopping $70,000 on the piece, later claiming the design was created with a special purpose.

One of the halos was dedicated to Brittany, while the second was created to represent Jax.

In April of 2021, the couple welcomed their first child, a baby boy named Cruz.

KATIE MALONEY & TOM SCHWARTZ

In July of 2015, Tom Schwartz proposed to Katie at Pearl's Rooftop in LA.

The TV star asked for forever with a small 2-carat pear-shaped champagne diamond set on a gold band designed by friend Kyle Chan.

The designer used 14-karat rose gold and added a pink sapphire on the band for an extra touch.

Despite its petite nature, Katie said she was “beyond happy” with the selection and added: “one of my and @twschwa closest friends designed this gorgeous ring and that makes it all the more special!"

LALA KENT & RANDALL EMMETT

Randall Emmett popped the question in September of 2018 while on a beautiful beach retreat.

The film producer got down on one knee with a gargantuan 6-carat diamond designed by Richie Rich from Leon Diamond in New York.

The TV personality claimed he worked with the designer for six months on the massive specimen, set on an 18K white gold ring.

The center stone was surrounded by a sparkling halo with diamonds on the band and Randall claimed the piece was worth $150,000.

However, after their explosive split, Lala recently went on a podcast to say that the ring was actually a fake, that it was previously a "brown" stone, and was only worth in the "thousands."

SCHEANA & MIKE SHAY

On the second season of Vanderpump Rules in 2013, Scheana announced her engagement to a man from high school with whom she became reconnected.

The pair went ring shopping together in Covina, saying of the purchase: "I was specific in that I wanted a thin band with a princess cut diamond in a halo setting."

However, her hunky hubby wanted to make it a little more special so he added diamonds that her father gifted her mother when she was born.

"Knowing I have part of my mom's ring just made my engagement ring that much more special!" she said at the time.

The square stone was set with a large halo and completed with two large diamond bands for a chunky feel.

However, their romance was short-lived and the couple divorced just three years later in 2017.

SCHEANA SHAY & BROCK DAVIES

On her second engagement, Scheana upgraded heavily as her fiancé and baby daddy Brock Davies presented her with a "rare" 12.74-Carat Pink Morganite ring.

The jaw-dropping jewelry also features a 14-karat white gold band adorned with 12 diamonds.

Friend of the cast Kyle Chan also designed the piece, saying: "It is a perfect rectangular cushion and this gem also possesses a rare fancy pink that reflects with insane fire.

"Morganite is all about Divine Love. Pure and simple, it is representative of unconditional love."

The pair welcomed their daughter Summer in April of 2021, and although they had planned a Bali ceremony for November of last year, their wedding was canceled due to the pandemic.

STASSI SCHROEDER & BEAU CLARK

Actor Beau Clark proposed to Stassi Schroeder with a family heirloom, a beautiful vintage ring that belonged to his grandfather in the 1940’s or 1950’s.

The Clark family treasure is a 3.5-carat ring featuring a European round-cut diamond surrounded by 24 smaller stones in a platinum setting.

With a price tag upwards of $100,000, Stassi said of her ring: “It’s beautiful. I feel like it’s like Cinderella’s glass slipper or something in a ring.
