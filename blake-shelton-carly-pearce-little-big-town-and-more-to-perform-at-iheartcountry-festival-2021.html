Blake Shelton, Carly Pearce, Little Big Town, And More To Perform At iHeartCountry Festival 2021

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, May 12, 2024

The iHeartCountry Festival is making its return for 2021! The much-anticipated music festival will unite some of the hottest names in country music under one roof.

Performers on the bill include Blake Shelton, Toby Keith, Little Big Town, Jake Owen, Lee Brice, Carly Pearce, Cole Swindell, and Chase Rice. Hosting the grand evening is none other than iHeartMedia’s top on-air Country personality, Bobby Bones.

After having to cancel last year’s show due to the COVID-19 pandemic, fans and performers are eager to attend this event and jump back into live music.

According to a press release, the iHeartCountry Festival is ready to deliver live music with an “iconic lineup.”

“The iHeartCountry Festival is one of Country music’s most-anticipated nights, and this year is especially exciting,” said Rod Phillips, Executive Vice President of Programming for iHeartCountry. “Live music is back, and we can’t wait to return to the Frank Erwin Center with this iconic lineup.”

The event, presented by Capital One, will take place on Oct. 30 at the Frank Erwin Center in Austin, Texas. Tickets go on sale Friday, July 30 at 12 p.m. CT at TexasBoxOffice.com.

Capital One Cardholders will have the opportunity to purchase pre-sale tickets beginning Tuesday, July 27 at 11 a.m. ET/10 a.m. CT through Thursday, July 29 at 11 a.m. ET/10 a.m. CT at iHeartRadio.com/CapitalOne. 

Those who are Capital One cardholders will also have the opportunity to add a Capital One Access Pass to their purchase. This pass will offer access to enjoy a VIP cardholder event at iHeartCountry Festival featuring a performance by Lee Brice, complimentary food and beverage, and more.

For those who can not make it to the in-person event, country music will be coming to you virtually! LiveXLive Media will exclusively stream the main stage event at livexlive.com. Additionally, iHeartMedia’s country music radio stations will broadcast the Festival live in their local markets and at iHeartRadio.com.
