Introduction :

  • Kristin Austin (maiden name Kristin Feres) is the wife of retired professional wrestler and WWE Hall of Famer, Stone Cold Steve Austin.
  • Beautiful and supportive, she has stood by her husband’s side on his journey outside of the WWE ring despite not always wanting to be in the spotlight.

Personal Life, Parents and Family Details :

  • Little is known about Kristin’s childhood or upbringing, and she herself is not very vocal about her early days or most of her adult life, to be honest.
  • She is more commonly known as the wife of WWE Hall of Famer and host of the Steve Austin Show, Stone Cold Steve Austin.
  • It is not certain where the couple met or when they got together but, they got hitched in 2009 and currently live together with their 3 dogs.
  • There has been no mention of whether or not Kristin has children of her own as the couple is incredibly private about details of their marriage and personal lives.
  • Stone Cold is, however, the father of four daughters, Jade Adams, Cassidy Williams, Loren Williams, and Stephanie Williams.

Kristin Austin Stone Cold Relationship :

  • When it comes to relationships and love in general, Kristin’s husband Steve has been through his fair share of tumultuous affairs.
  • Kristin is the fourth wife of the controversial wrestler, who has been arrested for battery and domestic violence in the past after he assaulted his former wife, WWE diva Debra Marshall.
  • His first marriage was to a woman named Kathryn Burrhus, whom he tied the knot in the November of 1990. But, after just a year and nine months of a tumultuous relationship, the couple filed for divorce in August of 1992.
  • Austin then married fellow wrestler Jeannie Clark in December 1992. They had two children, Cassidy and Stephanie, before splitting up officially in May 1999.
  • Austin married his third wife, Debra Marshall, in the same year on December 6 but after just 3 crazy years together, they divorced in February 2003.
  • Before meeting Kristin, Steve even dated an actress named Tess Broussard for a little more than a year and a half.
  • With his short temper, rowdy attitude, and quite a slew of substance abuse problems, Stone Cold’s wrestling persona seemed to follow him into his relationships, and he has not had the best of luck when it comes to love.
  • He has opened up a bit and talked about how meeting Kristin, a private and relatively drama-free woman, helped him turn his life around and embrace a quieter and more relaxed attitude.

Interesting Facts, Height and Trivia :

  • Kristin’s maiden name is Feres, so she sometimes goes by the name Kristin Feres. But, she is more commonly referred to by her married name, Austin.
  • She has made a few appearances on her husband’s podcast talking about hot topics like their brand new washing machine and life in general as a married couple.
  • She often accompanies her husband to various red carpet events and WWE events such as the Hall of Fame. Kristin also visits Steve’s fellow colleagues backstage at WWE events.
