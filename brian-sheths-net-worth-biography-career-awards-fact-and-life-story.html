Brian Sheths Net Worth, Biography, Career, Awards, Fact and Life Story

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 27, 2024
Net Worth:$1.1 Billion

Brian Sheth net worth: Brian Sheth is an American businessman and entrepreneur who has a net worth of $1.1 billion. Brian Sheth is best known for being the President and co-founder of Vista Equity Partners which formed in 2000. He graduated from the University of Pennsylvania where he met his wife. Sheth spent a year working for Deutsche Bank, a year at Goldman Sachs, and six months at Bain Capital. He was approached by Robert Smith who hired him at Goldman about starting up Vista. Sheth is the son of an Indian immigrant and a teacher and is chairman and the founding donor of Global Wildlife Conservation. He also serves as a director at over a dozen companies. Vista Equity Partners is a private equity firm that deals with software and technology businesses.
