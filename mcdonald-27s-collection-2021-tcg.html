McDonald's Collection 2021
Cards in set25
Release periodFrom February 9, 2021

The McDonald's Collection 2021 is a limited set of cards available from participating McDonald's restaurants in 2021 in celebration of the Pokémon 25th Anniversary. It began on February 9, 2021 in the United States and Trinidad and Tobago; March 2, 2021 in Suriname; March 11, 2021 in Germany; April 14, 2021 in France; April 26, 2021 in Canada; May 19, 2021 in the United Kingdom; June 3, 2021 in Sweden and Switzerland; June 9, 2021 in Belgium and the Netherlands; September 3, 2021 in Poland; September 9, 2021 in Australia and Brazil and October 15, 2021 in Spain.

Information

Like McDonald's Collections before it, the set features reprints with a new set symbol and numbering. The reprints all feature first partner Pokémon and come from various expansions from the Black & White, XY, Sun & Moon and Sword & Shield Series. Each card retains its original layout and has the 25th Anniversary logo in the bottom right corner of the card's artwork.

A pack of four cards was included in Happy Meals during the promotion, along with one of four novelty Pokémon items plus some stickers and a picture on which to use them, all contained within a cardboard envelope with a simle puzzle printed on it. Each card has a Non Holofoil and a Confetti Holofoil print, with one Confetti Holo included in each pack.

Set list

McDonald's Collection 2021

Languages this set is released in

The McDonald's Collection 2021 is released in English, German, French, Italian, Spanish and Portuguese. All cards are available as both non-holo and Confetti Holofoil.
