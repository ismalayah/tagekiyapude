How did Carol Alt become so famous?

They say, that beauty doesn’t mean happiness for a woman. But it is better to be miserable, when you are beautiful, then when you are not beautiful. No doubt, Carol Alt, whose fate was quite complicated, is incredibly beautiful and talented. She is popular as a model and an actress.

So, that’s how it happened.

Carol Alt was born in a middle class American family. She spent her childhood days in New York and then decided to enter Hofstra University. Her parents couldn’t pay for the girl’s education and she decided to earn some money as a waitress. While serving her tables, the chic girl was noticed by a scout of model agency and soon she starred on the covers of popular issues.

Later Carol Alt got a chance to try her hand as an actress too. She debuted in “My First Forty Years”. The Italian comedy of Carla Vanzina was liked by millions of people all over the world and became the winning ticket of the young actress Carol Alt.

Later she continued her successful career with such hits as “Private Parts”, “Amazon”, “Piper” etc.

Having succeeded as a model and actress, Carol started super successful career of a writer. She released the book “Eating in the Raw” and several years later published the second part of her healthy book.
