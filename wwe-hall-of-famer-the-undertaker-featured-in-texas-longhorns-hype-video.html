WWE Hall of Famer The Undertaker Featured in Texas Longhorns Hype Video

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 16, 2024

WWE has many legends, but few will ever compare to The Undertaker. The WWE Hall of Famer has also cemented himself in pop culture along the way.

The Texas Longhorns just dropped a new hype video, and it was very Undertaker heavy. The Phenom appears in the video, giving Texas fans something to be excited about.

The Undertaker has made his fandom of the Texas Longhorns no secret. He visited the team’s practice in the past, and was even presented with a pretty great gift.

The Undertaker’s legend in WWE will never die. At this point, you never know where his iconic image and song will pop up next. If anything, it certainly added a lot to this Texas Longhorns hype video.

WWE fans might never see The Undertaker wrestle again, but odds are, he will keep making his presence known, The man who used to never break kayfabe can be seen out and about in ways that we never thought possible before from The Deadman.
